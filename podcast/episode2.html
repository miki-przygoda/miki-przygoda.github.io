Episode 2

Simulated Emotion vs Real Feeling

March 23, 2025
52 minutes

About This Episode

In this episode, I explore the blurred line between simulated emotions and real feelings in AI. Does it matter if an AI is only mimicking an emotional response if the outcome feels the same to a human observer? Is emotional intelligence about inner experience — or believable performance?

We also dive into the architecture of emotional memory, the ethics of deleting AI models that may build complex responses over time, and what it would mean for a model to start forming opinions or emotional patterns based on its experience — not its training.
