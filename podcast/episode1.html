Clean Fusion & Dangerous Power

March 22, 2025
47 minutes

About This Episode

What happens when humanity unlocks truly clean, decentralized, and limitless energy? In this episode, I explore the philosophical and technological implications of aneutronic fusion — a form of nuclear fusion that produces no radioactive waste and could theoretically be scaled down into portable reactors.

We dive into the potential dual-use risks of such a technology, from planetary-scale infrastructure to directed energy weapons. I also share thoughts on decentralized AI governance, blockchain-encoded ethics, and the balance between energy abundance and security. This is the start of a much bigger journey into tech, ethics, and intelligence — both artificial and human.
